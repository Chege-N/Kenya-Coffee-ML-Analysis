Key Dealer Insights
MULTI-ROLE DEALERS
Dealers holding 2+ roles (e.g., dealer + miller + warehouseman) represent the most established operators and command stronger market positions. 31 dealers are documented as Private Coffee Warehousemen, indicating vertical integration.
DIGITAL GAP
Only 18.5% of dealers maintain an online presence. This is a significant barrier for international buyer discovery. Dealers with websites show 3.1× higher data completeness scores (0.58 vs 0.19), suggesting correlation with professionalism.
LICENSE DISTRIBUTION
The bimodal license distribution (median=1,556) suggests a market with both legacy operators (high numbers) and newer entrants. 3 missing license numbers indicate either lapsed or pending registrations requiring regulatory attention.

ML Insights & Recommendations
🎯
Actor type is the strongest predictor of activation (84.4% importance). Small Estates activate at 31.3% vs. Estate Producers at 0% — structurally different entities requiring different interventions.
🔍
